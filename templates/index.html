About Ladies Collections

Founded in 2007 by Sabiha Rahman in Koramangala, Bangalore, Ladies Collections specializes in exquisite one-gram gold, antique, and sterling silver jewelry that celebrates Indian craftsmanship.

With over 20+ years of expertise, we offer handpicked designs from traditional temple jewelry to contemporary pieces. Sabiha Rahman personally addresses every customer, ensuring you find the perfect piece that expresses your unique style.
